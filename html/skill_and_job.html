Skill and job

Our project still has some limitations and above all we always want to develop NB glasses into a useful and widely used product. Therefore, to bring NB glasses to the next stage, we need to recruit positions such as Hardware Engineer, Software Engineer, Product Development Engineer, and marketing (PR & Communication Executive). Hardware Engineer will mainly focus on developing the hardware and appearance of the glasses. Software Engineer will certainly develop the functions of the glasses. The product development engineer will research the market, and have the ability to work with Hardware Engineer and Software Engineer to improve and develop NB glasses in the most comprehensive way. To bring our products to market, we definitely need communication, which is also the reason we will hire a marketing person.

General Requirement

Skill

  • Ability to perform in a dynamic and ever-changing environment
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • The ability to work individually as well as in groups
  • Workplace autonomy and time management abilities
  • Always welcome feedback and provide more constructive feedback
  • Language: fluent in English communication and writing
  • Proficient in communicating and working in teams using platforms such as Microsoft 365, Github, etc.

Thinking

  • Innovative and creative thinking
  • Adjust to today's thinking

Detailed description

Hardware Enigineer

Responsibilities

  • Work with the research team and software team to complete and create new products in accordance with the project orientation.
  • Analyze, evaluate, and provide technical solutions for problems that the product appears.
  • Create product design documentation, hardware specs, and user manuals in accordance with standards.
  • Improve product efficiency and user preferences to boost corporate productivity.
  • Contact, and collaborate with suppliers.
  • Report working progress.

Qualifications

  • Graduated from IT majoring in electrical and electronics or equivalent fields.
  • Have a minimum of 2 years experience in a hardware engineering position.
  • Have a passion for electronic engineering, hardware design, circuit boards, and electronic components.

AI developer

Responsibilities

  • Develop artificial intelligence algorithms in accordance with project specifications.
  • Research and collect data to increase algorithm accuracy
  • Testing AI and code to ensure that it functions flawlessly
  • Collaborate with other departments when developing products.
  • Report on work progress and ensure that it is completed on time.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree with a major in artificial intelligence, information technology, applied mathematics, or other related field.
  • At least two years of experience working on AI development.
  • Responsible with a strong desire to learn.

Product Development Engineer

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for connecting with relevant people such as customers, project managers, software engineers, and hardware engineer product development teams to ensure product quality and features are in the right direction of the project's development.
  • Conduct market surveys to predict customer tastes and trends to improve products.
  • Create a product development roadmap and 1-3 year product development plan.
  • Research competitors on price, features, and technology to learn or develop new technology for the project.
  • Conduct product testing if necessary.
  • Work with marketing to provide commercial technology information.

Qualifications

  • Experience: at least 2 years in a related field.
  • Ability to effectively track and monitor projects.
  • Good communication and negotiation skills.
  • Have leadership ability in business development such as market analysis, strategy, and product development business planning.
  • Understand clearly the product manufacturing process and related information.

PR & Communication Executive

Responsibilities

    Building brand recognition (70%)
  • Responsible for the company's media, press, communication channels, social networks and website to protect the image and increase brand recognition in the media.
  • Ensure communication content is consistent and synchronized throughout according to the company's general direction.
  • Work collaboratively with other teams to ensure the quality of events, programs or customer outreach strategies.
  • Perform other tasks as assigned by the direct manager, report work progress on a daily/weekly/monthly basis or as requested by the direct manager.
    • Internal communication (30%):
  • Ensure internal communication is smooth, transparent, complete, accurate and timely.
  • In charge of image design, printing of internal and media publications according to the strategy and development stage of the project.
  • Organize celebration activities, birthday parties, anniversaries for project participants or event programs within the project framework as requested by management.

Qualifications

  • Graduated from university or higher majoring in Marketing, communications, journalism or related fields.
  • Have a minimum of 2 years working experience in a similar position.
  • Personality: enthusiastic, likes to communicate, proactive and eager to learn, not afraid to make mistakes to progress.
  • Capable of filming, taking photos and editing short videos.
  • Understand and grasp trends
  • Able to create trends and influence
  • Have artistic and aesthetic thinking
  • Ability to work in groups
  • Be determined with the assigned work to achieve the desired results.
