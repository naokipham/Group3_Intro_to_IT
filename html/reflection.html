Group Reflection

Phyu Phyu Shinn Thant (Zyra) Some tasks went well, such as dividing our sections and working on our individual sections. There are a few things that can be improved, including communication, and time management. Compared to our first assignment, I believe we lacked a bit more communication this time around since we were not able to have as many face-to-face meetings. Due to this reason, our time management was relatively flawed. One thing that came across as surprising was the web development section. In the beginning, according to the lecturer, we thought we would only need a basic HTML website with minimal design and user-friendliness, however, this turned out to be untrue if a group wanted to get HD, DI, or CR. We had to at least incorporate CSS for web design as well. Personally, I learned that groups, in general, need communication and different roles for each member to allow us to work more efficiently and effectively in a team environment.
