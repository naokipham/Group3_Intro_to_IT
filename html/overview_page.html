Project Description

Overview

Topic

To sum up, our product will perform translation back and forth to the user whichever language they are encountering with. The device can detect not only sound from people or electronic devices but also scans images that have foreign languages. Then, these images can be translated into your desired language, and you can have an option of appearing as a line of sentence on your glasses or audio version of translated information. Moreover, this device can bring balanced experiences to people, so it could scan the sign language to enhance the communication among people with disabilities such as blindness, deafness, and muted people. Sign language will be translated into a spoken language as reported by user choice and it is also turned into an audio file for blind people to listen to. To aid in interacting with people who use sign language, NBglasses has the capability to receive voice audio as the input source. It will correspondingly direct the user on how to reply using sign language in real-time. The regular version would be a more simplified model. The user can choose the type of translation they wish to receive such as a visual translation on the glass of your glasses (this will be combined with AR technology) or an audio translation in the form of an audio file. It can be used to translate content on a variety of products in different languages to make people lives easier.The outcome would be beyond our imagination and would solve a lot of problems of language barrier. The reason that we have to mention how difficult it is to communicate with people who do not have a common language is real life problem that a lot of people are facing right now. And there is an abundant amount of people who have to deal with this problem every day, including people with disabilities such as blindness and loss of hearing, because not everyone they come across in daily life can speak sign language, etc. with the help of this product, we will no longer have a problem of not understanding what the other person is trying talk or sign and it could be a smart glasses for people who have difficulty in reading.
