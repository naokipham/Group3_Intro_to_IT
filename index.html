NBglasses

Hello :] Welcome to NBglasses, a revolutionary pair of translation. Our aim with this project was to create something that will remove language barriers and improve communication. In the beginning, we started out with a normal pair of translation glasses that incorporated Augmented Reality and Computer Vision to translate images and audio. However, we realized that to truly remove language barriers, we needed think more inclusive. Hence, introducing our sign language translation feature. Our goal is to be as inclusive and groundbreaking as possible with our project and help unite the world.
